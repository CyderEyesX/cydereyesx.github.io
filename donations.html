Questions

Donation FAQ

Do donations provide benefits or returns?

No. Donations are voluntary support for educational outreach. They do not provide ownership, revenue share, profit rights, or guaranteed services.

Can I donate from any wallet?

Yes—just ensure you’re on the correct network for the address you’re using. If your wallet supports it, use QR scan and verify the address before sending.

Can I get confirmation?

If you need confirmation for your records, email us with the transaction hash at info@cydereyesx.com.

What’s the mission?

Help more people learn safer digital habits: avoid scams, protect accounts, improve privacy settings, and build foundational cybersecurity awareness—especially for beginners and bilingual learners.
