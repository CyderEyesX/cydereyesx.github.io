Why Donations Exist

Education Should Be Accessible

CyderEyesX donations are used to support free or low-cost educational initiatives, workshops, and learning resources focused on digital safety, responsible technology use, and cybersecurity fundamentals.

Contributions are never required. Donations do not grant ownership, investment rights, or preferential access to services.

Important Clarification

Donations are voluntary and support educational outreach only. CyderEyesX LLC is not a charity or investment platform. No returns, rewards, or guarantees are associated with contributions.
